I am a research scientist specialized in the collection and analysis of digital data for social research and public policy.

I currently serve as director of the Data Lab of the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority, a team tasked with building “innovative, data-driven solutions that bridge research and practice.” We help Metro operate more efficiently, adapt to change and serve the community better every day.


Vitae →

Before joining WMATA, I was the associate director of Data Labs, the computational social research team of Pew Research Center. I also worked as senior data science manager at Westat, a research services company. I started my career as statistician in the Scientific Research Group of YouGov. I received my MA and Ph.D. in Politics from New York University.

Originally from Galicia (Spain), I am currently based in Washington, D.C.
